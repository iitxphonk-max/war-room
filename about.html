About WAR ROOM

WAR ROOM was built for students who are tired of distractions, inconsistent study habits, and feeling unproductive.

It is not just a Pomodoro timer. It is a discipline system.

Why WAR ROOM Exists

Most productivity tools track time. Very few build momentum.

WAR ROOM combines focused sessions, daily streaks, and a gamified XP system to make discipline measurable. Every completed session builds progress. Every streak builds identity.

Who It Is For

WAR ROOM is for serious students — whether preparing for competitive exams, university studies, skill building, or any long-term goal that requires focus.

The Vision

The goal is simple: Create an environment where discipline wins. Where progress is visible. Where consistency becomes addictive.
