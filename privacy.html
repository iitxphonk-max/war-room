Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

WAR ROOM is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use this website.

1. Information We Collect

WAR ROOM does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or passwords.

2. Local Storage

The application uses your browser's local storage to save session data, streak counts, XP levels, and planner notes. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

3. Cookies and Future Advertising

In the future, WAR ROOM may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdSense. These services may use cookies to display relevant advertisements. Users will be able to control cookie preferences through their browser settings.

4. Data Security

Since all productivity data is stored locally within your browser, you maintain full control over your information.

5. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as features evolve, including the addition of accounts or online services.

6. Contact

Currently, WAR ROOM does not collect contact information. If communication channels are added in the future, this section will be updated accordingly.
