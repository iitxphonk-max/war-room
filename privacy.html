Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

WAR ROOM respects your privacy and is committed to transparency regarding how data is handled when you use this website.

1. Information Collected

WAR ROOM does not require user registration and does not directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or passwords.

This website uses Google Analytics (GA4), a web analytics service provided by Google, to understand website usage and improve performance.

2. Analytics and Usage Data

Google Analytics may automatically collect non-personally identifiable data including:

  • Device and browser information
  • Pages visited and session duration
  • Country-level geographic region
  • Interaction events

This data is aggregated and used solely to improve user experience.

3. Local Storage

WAR ROOM stores productivity-related data (sessions, streaks, XP, planner notes) locally in your browser. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

4. Cookies

Google Analytics may use cookies to analyze traffic patterns. You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Future Advertising

WAR ROOM may use advertising services such as Google AdSense in the future. These services may use cookies or similar technologies to display relevant ads.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the platform evolves.
